Order Like a Local at Your Favorite Hawaiian Plate Lunch Spot

Why Learn Pidgin for Plate Lunch?

Walking into a local Hawaiian plate lunch spot can be intimidating if you don't know the lingo. But mastering just a few essential Hawaiian Pidgin phrases will make you sound like a local, earn respect from the staff, and help you get exactly what you want from your meal.

Whether you're visiting Hawaii or you're a new resident, this guide will teach you 15 authentic phrases you'll hear (and need to use) at every plate lunch counter from Honolulu to Hilo.
